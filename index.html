About Me

Hi, I’m Sarath, a UI/UX designer and web developer. I’m a second-year student of Bachelor of Engineering in Robotics and Automation at Anna University, where I have learned how to design and program robots, sensors, and controllers. I have also applied my knowledge and skills to various projects, such as building a robotic arm that can play chess, creating a smart home system that can control lights and temperature, and developing a drone that can deliver packages.

Some of the challenges I have faced in my work include designing for different screen sizes and devices, ensuring accessibility and usability for diverse users, and testing and debugging code and prototypes. I have overcome these challenges by using responsive design principles, conducting user research and testing, and using online tools and resources to troubleshoot and improve my work.

Some of the successes I have achieved in my work include winning a hackathon with my team, receiving a prizes from a leading tech company, and getting featured on a popular design blog. These successes have motivated me to keep learning and growing as a designer and developer, and to share my work and insights with others.

My future goals include graduating with honors, landing a job at a reputable company or agency, and launching my own products or services. I also want to learn more about artificial intelligence, machine learning, and blockchain, and how they can be integrated with UI/UX design and web development. I hope to make a positive impact with my work, not only for myself, but also for my clients, users, and society.
